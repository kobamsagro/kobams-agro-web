# Product Highlights Feature Guide

## Overview
The Product Highlights feature allows you to showcase key benefits and value propositions of your products in an interactive card format on product detail pages. This benefits-oriented approach helps customers understand why your product is the right choice.

## How to Add Product Highlights in the CMS

1. **Navigate to Products Collection**
   - Go to your Payload CMS admin panel
   - Click on "Products" in the sidebar
   - Select a product to edit or create a new one

2. **Go to Product Highlights Tab**
   - Click on the "Product Highlights" tab
   - Click "Add Highlight" to create a new benefit highlight card

3. **Fill in Highlight Details**

   ### Required Fields:
   - **Benefit Title**: The main benefit heading (e.g., "Extended Shelf Life", "Premium Quality", "Export Ready")
   - **Short Description**: A brief one-line description of the benefit
     - Example: "Stays fresh for up to 24 months under proper storage"
   - **Detailed Benefits**: Add at least 2 specific advantages (click "Add Benefit" for each)
     - Examples:
       - "Reduces waste and storage costs"
       - "Maintains nutritional value throughout storage"
       - "Minimizes spoilage and product loss"

   ### Optional Fields:
   - **Additional Information**: Extra context or certifications
     - Example: "Our products meet international standards including ISTA, ISO, and HACCP certifications"

4. **Save the Product**
   - Click "Save" to publish your changes
   - The highlights will appear on the product detail page immediately

## Example Highlights (Benefits-Oriented)

### Example 1: Extended Shelf Life
- **Benefit Title**: Extended Shelf Life
- **Short Description**: Stays fresh for up to 24 months under proper storage
- **Detailed Benefits**:
  - Reduces waste and storage costs
  - Maintains nutritional value throughout storage
  - Minimizes spoilage and product loss
  - Provides flexibility in inventory management
- **Additional Information**: Our low moisture content (<12%) meets ISTA international standards, ensuring optimal preservation

### Example 2: Premium Quality Assurance
- **Benefit Title**: Premium Quality Assurance
- **Short Description**: Rigorous quality control ensures consistent excellence
- **Detailed Benefits**:
  - 98%+ purity with no stones or impurities
  - Meets international quality standards (ISO, HACCP)
  - Consistent product specifications in every batch
  - Enhanced market value and customer satisfaction
- **Additional Information**: Every batch undergoes multi-stage quality testing and certification

### Example 3: Export Ready
- **Benefit Title**: Export Ready
- **Short Description**: Fully compliant with international export regulations
- **Detailed Benefits**:
  - Pre-certified for major international markets
  - Complete documentation and traceability
  - Meets phytosanitary requirements
  - Reduces customs delays and complications
- **Additional Information**: We handle all export documentation including NAFDAC, phytosanitary certificates, and certificates of origin

### Example 4: Cost-Effective Packaging
- **Benefit Title**: Cost-Effective Packaging
- **Short Description**: Optimized packaging reduces shipping costs
- **Detailed Benefits**:
  - Multiple packaging options for different needs
  - Reduces transport weight and costs
  - Protects product integrity during shipping
  - Eco-friendly and sustainable materials
- **Additional Information**: Available in 25kg, 50kg bags and bulk containers

## User Experience

### On the Product Page:
1. Highlights appear as cards in a 3-column grid (responsive on mobile)
2. Each card shows the icon, title, and subtitle
3. Users can click "Click for details" to see more information

### In the Modal:
1. Full details are displayed in a popup
2. Shows all benefits with checkmarks
3. Displays testing method (if provided)
4. Shows "Why It Matters" section (if provided)
5. Users can close the modal by clicking the X or outside the modal

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Focus on Customer Benefits**: Think about what matters to your buyers
   - Instead of "Moisture Content < 12%", use "Extended Shelf Life"
   - Instead of "Purity 98%", use "Premium Quality Assurance"
   - Focus on outcomes, not just specifications

2. **Write Compelling Descriptions**: Make it clear and customer-focused
   - Good: "Stays fresh for up to 24 months under proper storage"
   - Better: "Save money and reduce waste with our extended shelf life"

3. **List Specific Benefits**: Be concrete about the advantages
   - "Reduces waste and storage costs" (specific)
   - "Meets international standards" (credible)
   - "Minimizes spoilage" (practical)

4. **Add 3-5 Highlights**: This creates a balanced display without overwhelming users

5. **Think Like Your Customer**: What problems are they trying to solve?
   - Importers care about: compliance, documentation, reliability
   - Processors care about: quality, consistency, specifications
   - Retailers care about: shelf life, packaging, market appeal

## Technical Notes

- Highlights are optional - if no highlights are added, the section won't display
- The section appears before "Quality Specifications" on the product detail page
- Changes are reflected immediately after saving (with on-demand revalidation)
- The feature is fully responsive and works on all devices
